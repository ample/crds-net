Undefeated

Perfect for: Anyone exploring what God says about your spiritual and physical health.
How Long: 8 Weeks

We believe God has things to say about our spiritual and physical health. This group is for any adult who is looking to explore this truth in community. This will include table discussion, as well as activities, over an 8-week period.
