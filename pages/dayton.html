We meet weekly at Bellbrook Middle School. We serve communities in the Greater Dayton area such as Bellbrook, Centerville, Springboro, Kettering, Beavercreek, and other surrounding neighborhoods. We started with a small group of people and are now a vibrant and growing community. So where are we headed? Only God really knows, but we're excited for the journey. And you’re invited to join us.

Come as you are

Seriously. This is a real place for real people. Regardless of what you think about God, you are welcome here. In a nutshell, you can wear jeans, or dress up if you want. The coffee is free, so grab a cup before you sit down. The music is great and you’ll hear straight up Biblical truth that you can apply to your everyday life. The rest is up to you.

Meet Jeff Crull

Jeff is the Dayton Community Pastor. He spent 27 years at Procter & Gamble before taking a leap of faith to work in ministry. He spent five years at City Gospel Mission working with underserved youth in Cincinnati. He also served as a board member for the Cincinnati Youth Collaborative, and as an advisory committee member at Most Valuable Kids. In June 2017, Jeff and his wife Lisa moved to Dayton to help launch Crossroads. Jeff loves Jesus, his family, and pretty much any cookie.
