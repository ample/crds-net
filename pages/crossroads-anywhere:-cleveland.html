Welcome to Crossroads!

About 20 years ago, we started a church for our friends who didn’t like church. We wanted a place where you can come as you are, and you could actually look forward to being there—whether you’re questioning if God even exists, or you’re a committed Christ-follower. We’ve been in Cincinnati for all that time, but through this crazy thing called Crossroads Anywhere, we’re reaching cities all over the nation and the world.
