Transforming Betrayal

Perfect for: Women whose husbands struggle with pornography or inappropriate sexual behaviors.
When/How Long: Weekly in two parts

Questions? Email us at groups@crossroads.net

This is a two-part group for women who have experienced the pain of their spouse's or partners betrayal through infidelity, pornography, or sexual addiction of any kind.

Part A

Duration: 18 weeks

We'll explore how to manage life in the earlier parts of discovery, seeking to understand what sexual addiction is, and supporting each other as we process. Each week we'll explore a new healing topic. Some of the topics for Part A include:

  • Safe people
  • Coping
  • Non-negotiable boundaries
  • Choices
  • Anger

Oakley
Thursdays, 7-8:30pm
Childcare is available with one week advance registration.

*Attendance for every week is highly encouraged as the safety and consistency of the group is very important. There is required homework and journaling.

If you would like to participate in an upcoming group, sign up here. You can also check out an Introduction Meeting in the meantime (see below).

Part B

Duration: 12 weeks

Part B is a deeper dive into how we process life. Some of the topics in Part B are losses, fear and childhood influences.

Oakley
Thursdays, 7-8:30pm
Childcare is available with one week advance registration.

*Part B is invite-only. You must go through Part A first. Attendance for every week is highly encouraged as the safety and consistency of the group is very important. There is required homework and journaling.

Introduction Meetings

Monthly introduction meetings are for women who have just discovered the betrayal. Get materials to help until Part A is available to attend. Just coming to an info session does not commit you to the group.
