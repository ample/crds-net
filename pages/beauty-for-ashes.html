Beauty for Ashes

Perfect for: Women seeking emotional healing from sexual abuse, rape or sexual assault.
How Long: 11 weeks

Learn about the effects of abuse, stages of healing, authentic identity and how to find freedom with God in a safe place. The group size is 15 women with complete anonymity and confidentiality.
