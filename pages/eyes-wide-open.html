Eyes Wide Open

Perfect for: Women struggling with food and body-image.
How Long: 10 weeks

Because of the valuable content, knowledge, and resources provided, this group will cost $50 to participate. You will be contacted via email after registration with ways to collect payment.

Get out of the cycle of negative thinking and destructive behaviors when it comes to food and body-image.

This group will launch again in 2019. Would you like to know when signups go live for the next round? Great! Let us know HERE.

This 10-week healing program will help you:

  • Toss out toxic thoughts
  • Replace food and body-image lies with biblical truth
  • Break free from disordered eating
  • Love yourself for who you are
  • Discover your true identity
  • Grow closer to God
  • Develop authentic relationships with other women
  • And more!

What to Expect:

  • 10-week commitment
  • Weekly meetings (2 hours)
  • Homework (w/light reading & questions)
  • Safe/loving environment to share and be yourself
  • Biblical truth to replace worldly lies
  • Support and encouragement from leaders and other participants
  • Practical tools to help you learn, grow, and live in freedom

Group size is limited and advanced sign-up is mandatory. Some locations have a waiting list so be sure to contact us right away to ensure placement.

*This group is not intended to take the place of medical or psychological care.
